About Thinkwerke

European delivery experience for regulated industries

Thinkwerke is led by a group of passionate engineers with deep European delivery experience across information security, cloud security architecture, and software development. The team brings 15+ years of experience from organisations such as IBM, Oracle, and Red Hat, along with consulting work for Microsoft, Tesco, and AWS partners across Europe.

Focus: strengthening trust and operational resilience for regulated and core-industry organisations operating in vendor-hosted environments. Thinkwerke turns complex requirements into executable controls, audit-ready evidence, and decision-grade visibility — enabling leadership to confidently scale products, enter regulated markets, and respond to customers, auditors, and regulators without slowing engineering. The result is reduced compliance friction, faster assurance cycles, and an operating posture built to withstand scrutiny.

Problems we fix

Business Outcomes for Regulated Industries

Security and compliance become expensive when they create uncertainty, slow delivery, or block commercial progress. Thinkwerke is engaged when organisations need clarity and execution — fast. We enable core industries to focus on marketing, sales, and business expansion while we remove the compliance, engineering, and regulatory blockers affecting business objectives.

Regulation vs engineering deadlock

Policies exist, but delivery teams don’t know what “good” looks like in cloud and CI/CD. We translate requirements (ISO 27001, NIS2, CRA, DORA, AI Act, GDPR) into practical controls and implementation patterns.

Revenue blocked by assurance

Enterprise deals slow down due to questionnaires, tenders, and security reviews. We build a reusable evidence library and clear narratives so sales cycles become predictable.

Vendor-hosted cloud accountability gaps

Running on vendor-provided hosting (public cloud / managed services) creates shared responsibility confusion. We map controls to services and show how to prove your side with audit-ready evidence.

Tools exist, operating model doesn’t

SIEM/CSPM/scanners are deployed but ownership, workflow, and reporting are missing. We build the operating model: detections, vulnerability lifecycle, governance, and dashboards.

Security slows delivery

Controls feel heavy and get bypassed. We embed security into DevSecOps using automation and policy-as-code so teams can ship faster without increasing risk.

Supply-chain & SBOM becoming a blocker

CRA expectations (SBOM, OSS license governance, vulnerability handling) need traceable workflows. We implement evidence-producing pipelines that stand up to audits and customer scrutiny.
