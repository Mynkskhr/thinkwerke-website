About Thinkwerke

European market delivery experience for regulated and core industries

Thinkwerke brings deep European market delivery experience to organisations operating in regulated and core industries. The team is led by senior engineers with backgrounds spanning information security, cloud architecture, and software development, built over 15+ years at organisations such as IBM, Oracle, and Red Hat, alongside consulting engagements with Microsoft, Tesco, and AWS partners across Europe and USA.

Focus: strengthening trust and operational resilience in vendor-hosted environments. Thinkwerke translates complex regulatory and customer expectations into executable controls, audit-ready evidence, and decision-grade visibility. This enables leadership teams to scale products, enter regulated markets, and respond confidently to customers, auditors, and regulators without slowing engineering or delivery.

The result is lower compliance friction, faster assurance cycles, and an operating model designed to withstand external scrutiny.

Our Expert Architects & Engineers hold Credentials to showcase there depth and knowledge with CompTIA SecurityX Architect, AWS Solutions Architect – Professional, AWS Security Specialty, CISM (ISACA), ISO/IEC 27001 Lead Implementer (TÜV SÜD), and NIS2 & DORA trained professional.

What we deliver

Solutions & Services

Thinkwerke delivers outcomes through a small number of well-defined capability pillars:

Compliance translated into execution

  • Mapping ISO 27001, NIS2, CRA, DORA, GDPR and customer requirements to real cloud and CI/CD controls
  • Clear separation of policy, implementation, and proof
  • Evidence workflows built for audits, tenders, and customer assurance

Vendor-hosted accountability

  • Operationalising shared responsibility models for public cloud and managed services
  • Control mapping to cloud-native services, identity, logging, and monitoring
  • Defensible narratives and evidence showing what you own and how it’s proven

Secure software delivery (SSLM)

  • Secure pipelines covering SAST, SCA/SBOM, DAST, IaC and container checks
  • Vulnerability lifecycle with ownership, SLAs, workflow, and traceability
  • Artifact integrity, attestation, and policy-as-code embedded into CI/CD

Security operating models

  • Cloud-centric SOC and SIEM modernisation
  • CSPM / ASPM / CNAPP integrated into governance and reporting
  • Dashboards and metrics aligned to executives, auditors, and regulators

All delivery is designed to be auditable, repeatable, and owned by your teams — not dependent on long-term external presence.
